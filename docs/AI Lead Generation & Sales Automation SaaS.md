# AI Lead Generation & Sales Automation SaaS

## Product Requirements Document (PRD)

# 1. Product Overview

## Product Name

LeadFlow AI (Working Title)

## Product Vision

LeadFlow AI is an AI-powered Lead Generation, CRM, Outreach, and Sales Automation platform that helps agencies, freelancers, SaaS companies, and sales teams discover leads, personalize outreach, automate follow-ups, manage pipelines, and convert prospects into customers from a single platform.

Unlike traditional CRMs, LeadFlow AI combines AI, workflow automation, website analysis, content generation, and multi-channel outreach into one unified SaaS platform.
